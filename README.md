# Maintenance Contract Health Architecture

> Enterprise architecture and reporting framework for monitoring the health, profitability, utilization, and renewal performance of maintenance contracts.

---

## 📖 Overview

The **Maintenance Contract Health Architecture** provides a structured approach to analyzing maintenance contract performance by consolidating contract, equipment, service, billing, customer, and operational data into a unified analytics framework.

The objective is to provide proactive visibility into contract health, identify revenue opportunities, reduce contract leakage, and improve customer retention through data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor maintenance contract lifecycle and health.
- Identify contracts nearing expiration.
- Detect low-utilization and high-risk contracts.
- Improve contract renewal rates.
- Track contract profitability and service costs.
- Measure customer engagement and equipment coverage.
- Enable proactive account management.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture Scope

The architecture integrates data from multiple operational systems to provide a single source of truth for maintenance contract reporting and analytics.

### Data Domains

| Domain | Purpose |
|----------|----------|
| Contract Management | Contract details, terms, coverage, expiration |
| Equipment Master | Equipment inventory and asset information |
| Service Operations | Work orders, maintenance activities, repairs |
| Billing & Revenue | Contract billing and revenue realization |
| Customer Data | Customer ownership and account hierarchy |
| Sales & CSC Structure | Sales ownership and regional accountability |

## 📈 Recommended KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Contract Renewal Rate | Percentage of contracts renewed |
| Contract Expiration Rate | Contracts approaching expiration |
| Revenue Retention Rate | Retained recurring revenue |
| PM Compliance Rate | Preventive maintenance completion percentage |
| Service Cost Ratio | Service cost vs contract revenue |
| Contract Utilization Rate | Actual usage against expected service activity |
| Customer Retention Rate | Customer contract continuation rate |
| Equipment Coverage % | Covered equipment vs total equipment |
